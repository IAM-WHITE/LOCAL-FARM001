Our Story

Founded in 2005 in the lush farmlands of Ado Ekiti, Green Harvest has grown from a small family farm to a leading agricultural exporter in Nigeria.

Our commitment to sustainable farming practices and premium quality produce has earned us recognition across Africa, Europe, and Asia.

With over 500 hectares of farmland and state-of-the-art processing facilities, we deliver the authentic taste of Nigeria to the world.

Our Premium Products
